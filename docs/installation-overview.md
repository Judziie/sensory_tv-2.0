# Sensory TV 2.0 Remote - Installation Overview

## Product Introduction
**Sensory TV 2.0** is a smart remote control designed to enhance accessibility and ease of use for individuals who are hearing and/or visually impaired. It features voice recognition, braille navigation buttons, and a built-in microphone and camera system to improve television interaction.

## Purpose and Application
Sensory TV empowers users through its braille navigation buttons, voice recognition, and video recording features. It is suitable for households, long-term care facilities, and educational environments.

## Before You Start
- Power on the TV.
- Ensure Sensory TV's battery level is normal or above normal.
- Verify the television supports IR or Bluetooth connectivity.
- Format TV to the correct input.

## How to Pair Sensory TV with a Television

### Pairing by Bluetooth
1. Turn on the television using the original remote or power button.
2. Search for Bluetooth pairing:  
   **Settings → Connections → Bluetooth → Add Device**  
   (Path may vary by brand.)
3. Turn on Sensory TV: Press and hold the **Power** button for 3 seconds. The LED blinks green.
4. Enable pairing mode: Press and hold the **Connect** button (above LED indicators) for 5 seconds. LEDs blink blue.
5. Select **Sensory TV** on the television from the list of available devices.
6. Wait for confirmation: LED turns solid blue and voice feedback announces **“paired”**.
