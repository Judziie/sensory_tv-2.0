# Sensory TV 2.0: The Remote of the Future

**Formal Definition:**  
Sensory TV 2.0 is a smart remote control designed to enhance accessibility and ease of use for hearing and/or visually impaired individuals through its audio and video assistance features.

**General Description:**  
The remote has a matte-black finish and navigation buttons. Braille keys help visually impaired users. A microphone and camera are embedded at the top, LED lights and a speaker provide feedback.

**Purpose & Application:**  
Assistive device improving TV usability for hearing/visually impaired users. Useful in homes, care facilities, and educational environments.

### Major Component Parts
1. Braille Navigation Keypad  
2. Voice Recognition Microphone  
3. Video Recording Camera  
4. IR & Bluetooth Transmitter  
5. On-Device Speaker  
6. Rechargeable Battery Pack  
7. LED Indicator Lights
