# Pairing Sensory TV with a Television

## Before you start
1. Power on the TV.
2. Ensure the remote battery is charged.
3. Confirm TV supports IR or Bluetooth.

## Pairing by Bluetooth
1. On TV: Settings → Connections → Bluetooth → Add Device.
2. On Sensory TV: Press and hold Power 3s (LED blinks green).
3. Press and hold Connect 5s (LED blinks blue).
4. Select Sensory TV on TV.
5. Confirm pairing when LED turns solid blue and voice says "paired".
