# Sensory TV Remote Specifications

## Major Component Parts

### 1. Braille Navigation Keypad
- Traditional navigation buttons supported with braille dots.

### 2. Voice Recognition Microphone
- Positioned at the top; receives and processes spoken commands.

### 3. Video Recording Camera
- Embedded at the top; records within a 3-metre radius when activated.

### 4. Infrared (IR) & Bluetooth Transmitter
- Supports traditional and smart TVs.

### 5. On-Device Speaker
- Provides voice feedback for commands and notifications.

### 6. Rechargeable Battery Pack
- Powers the remote; battery checked via voice command.

### 7. LED Indicator Lights
- Colour-coded lights near the tail for device status.
