# Introducing Sensory TV 2.0

Sensory TV 2.0 is smart remote with voice control, braille navigation buttons, and a built-in microphone and camera recording system, designed to support users with hearing and/ or visual impairments.

Sensory TV 2.0 was created as an assistive device aimed at improving the television user experience for individuals who are hearing and/or visually impaired. It empowers users through its braille navigation buttons, voice recognition and video recording features while watching or interacting with the paired television.
